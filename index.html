The RK Legacy

A journey from a humble food cart to a culinary landmark.

Driven by Passion

The RK story began in 1999 when Ashok Kumar & Ram Shankar started a small food cart to support his family. With a natural talent for authentic Andhra flavors, he built a legacy of trust and taste.

From those grassroots origins, we've remained committed to serving high-quality, fresh food at reasonable prices. Every dish today carries the same dedication that started on that humble cart decades ago.

"Freshness and quality aren't just standards – they are our family's promise."
